About This Project

Who am I?

Hi! I'm IEatLeeks, a UTAU user and creator who loves experimenting with new voices and characters. I started this project to share my voicebanks with the community and to help others bring their music to life!

What is this site?

This site is a distribution centre for all my UTAU voicebanks. Here, you can find downloads, samples, and information about each character. My goal is to make it easy and fun for you to discover and use my voicebanks in your own projects.
